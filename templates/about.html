About IMIK

Ibaybay mo, Isasalin ko, is a web application that allows
transliteration between Latin script and Baybayin, an ancient Philippine script.
It employs symbols from the modernized version while adhering to the pre-Hispanic
rules of the script. Explore the beauty and cultural richness of Baybayin,
seamlessly blending tradition with contemporary elements.

Our Mission

Our goal with this project is to develop a web application that transliterates
between the Latin script and Baybayin script. We aim to apply concepts from automata
theory, specifically pushdown automata, which is incorporated in both transliterations.
Our objective is to enhance appreciation and understanding of the ancient Baybayin
script by making it accessible for people to read and write this script from our past.
